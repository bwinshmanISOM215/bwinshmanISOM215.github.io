Herbal Tea

Heath Facts


1. It Can Help You Relax

Herbal tea is popularly known for its calming effects and has been used for centuries as the focal point of social events and unwinding after a long day. Herbal tea has no caffine in it, therefore, it makes a wonderful drink before bed.


2. Improves Your Digestive System

Herbal teas have phenols that can strengthen both stomach muscles and the muscles in your esophagus, which can reduce acid reflux and heartburn symptoms. Teas also enable your digestive tract to more easily absorb nutrients.


3. Anti-ageing

The antioxidants found in herbal teas have shown to aid in slowing down the ageing process. They prevent free radical damage and reduce the ageing of cells in the body. This makes your skin and hair look and feel younger.
